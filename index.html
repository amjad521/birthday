Papa, from the day I was born, you've been my first hero, my safest place, and my biggest cheerleader. Every time life got hard, you were the one who showed up, no questions asked. You taught me to be brave by being brave yourself, and to love fiercely by loving all of us the same way. I hope one day I can be even a small part of the parent you've been to me. Thank you for every quiet sacrifice, every late night, and every hug that made the whole world feel safe again. I love you more than words can really hold, Papa.
